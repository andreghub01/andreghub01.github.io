Synopsis :

Solo Leveling. Ten years ago, after the opening of the "Gate" which connected the real world to the monster world, some of the ordinary, everyday people were given the power to hunt monsters inside the Gate. They're known as "Hunters," but not all Hunters are mighty. My name is Sung Jin-Woo, a Hunter with an E-rank. I'm someone in the lowest of dungeons, the "World's Weakest," who will lose his life. With no skills to show, I barely earned the money needed by fighting in low-level dungeons
